it's been a while since i wrote.

the light is changing now. it's softer, thinner, like it's trying not to wake anyone up. 
i noticed today that the neighbor started planting those blue flowers again. the ones that 
only bloom for a week and then disappear like they were never there.

i send these letters because sometimes a poem is too heavy, and a text is too light. 
this is the middle ground.

i hope you're finding your own middle ground today.

talk soon,
seven
