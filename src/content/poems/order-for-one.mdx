waiter waiter,
i'd like to cancel the hours i gave him,
also all the love that i had
oh, it's already ready?
can you box it up for a takeaway?

do you mind packing up all the leftovers as well?
he left early without a word;
i guess it was the taste of me
that was too much for him
can you blend it out with a pinch of sass? thank you.
no, no ravioli for two today,
just an iced coffee and the old me, thanks.

i'm sorry, but the me before him
was cooked just right
crispy edges, perfect sweet and salty,
a little underdone in places,
but, oh, it had flavour.
can you ask the chef to share the recipe
if that's not too much?

and what's today's special meal?
self-respect?
yeah, i would like a side of that , too.
and could you throw in a little hope to help me cope?

oh, and waiter
if you find any crumbs of who i was
under that old table ,
sweep them up.
i'll be waiting right here,
until i am crispy and salty again.
thank you.
