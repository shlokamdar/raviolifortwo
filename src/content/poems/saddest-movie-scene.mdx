this is the saddest movie scene playing,
in pin drop silence –
IV drips into your hand
the oxygen tank bubbles and fills your lungs.
my knees are bruised from praying to God,
my prayers have stacked up to His door–
yet there is no flower in the vase,
yet there is no light in this house.

the nurses laugh at the station
as if the world outside our bed is still moving.
in pin drop silence covered in grey walls–
i ask you to pray for me
because God always listens to you
because without you, i don't know where to be

this is the saddest movie scene playing
 i stand by your bed,
pretending to be stronger than you
praying, praying, praying –

i tell you, this is just until tomorrow,
my prayer has reached on His desk –
and he will paint our walls yellow again.
