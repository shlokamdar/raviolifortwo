she tells me i hold onto things so
tight they suffocate and shatter

she urges me to let it breathe,
to hold it gently,

and now, i am always holding things i love
at the loosest grip of my pinky finger,
i am always dropping things i love on the floor
before they break in my warm arms, leaving a scar

i am always touching things i love
from five feet afar,

aching for warmth, afraid of the flame.
