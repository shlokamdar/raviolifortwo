the world spins faster on thursdays -

  i trace the lines on my fingers in the mornings
  and watch the milk boil all over the stove.

  i sip on my coffee as i watch lovebirds make nests
in the spaces between the branches of a tree.
i take the busiest train and watch
the girl fall in love with someone on the screen.
i watch the waves meet the shore,
the wind flirting with the leaves.

i take the same busy train back home
and watch nervous hands finding
solace in their lover's embrace.
while i sit back, with so much love in my heart,
but no hand to fill in the s pa ces between my fingers
no forehead to plant a kiss and watch flowers bloom.
     everyone is falling in love and i am falling behind
