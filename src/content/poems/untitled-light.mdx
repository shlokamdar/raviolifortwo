you carried me through the dark nights
    towards this world
and laid my heart like a closed treasure box
in my hands. 'open it,' you said, 'when it was time,'
and I did.
I moved through mountains, through unswimmable oceans.
I carried your prayers in my prayers
mumma, before you grew out your favourite pants
were you also scared?
were you also lost in this maze?
          once, you sat beside me in the park
and told a story of a beautiful place of light
I know now I am that light
your prayers in my prayers will lead me here.
