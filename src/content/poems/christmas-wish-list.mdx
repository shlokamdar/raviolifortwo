after Trev Cimenski @of_a_hound

one. an hourglass that never runs out of sand,
where time doesn't end when
our stomachs are full of laughs
and we cry out in joy.

two. a lot of sushi.

three. a lot of inexpensive sushi.

four. a huge lost and found window,
that takes me to all the things I lost,
all the people i lost.

five. a stronger shoulder
to hold it all bravely,
to let them lay their heads gently.

six. warmer hands holding me.

seven. love,
brave love. love always standing at the door.
love singing with me. love loving me.

eight. happy poems,
from days I will live and
look at the sun to take it all in.

nine. more love.

ten. many more smiles.
