with boxes checked in your careful handwriting

and a bottle of cold ginger tea, half empty.
because you always drink just enough to say you did.

and some untouched yogurt and ice cream in the freezer
just in case you'll feel hot and want it later

half eaten apples and some expired milk,
leftover soup - your recipe that always needed more salt

some lemons that life threw, rolling in the egg tray
and injections lined up after dinner

but there will always be space for more birthday cakes
the kind we'll have for breakfast

and always,  space for lemons and lemonades
for every tomorrow waiting for us
